About Arrilan

Arrilan is a business automation consultancy based in Barbados, dedicated to helping growing businesses scale without limits. We specialize in designing and implementing custom automation solutions that solve real-world operational challenges for industries such as restaurants, cafes, marketing agencies, and retail. Our mission is to eliminate the "manual everything" approach that often hinders business growth. By replacing disconnected tools and chaotic workflows with enterprise-grade automation, we empower business owners to reclaim their time, reduce costs, and focus on what matters most: expansion and customer satisfaction.

What We Do

Specialized Industry Solutions: From "Restaurant Command Centers" with integrated POS systems to automated concierge services for the hospitality sector, we build tools tailored to specific industry needs.

Custom Architecture: For unique business problems, we design scalable, bespoke automation systems that integrate seamlessly into existing workflows.

End-to-End Support: Our three-step process—Discovery, Custom Design, and Launch—ensures that every client receives a solution that is fully optimized and supported as their business evolves.

At Arrilan, we believe that running a business shouldn't feel like a constant struggle against inefficient systems. Serving clients from Barbados to the rest of the world, we are committed to delivering powerful automation that actually works.
